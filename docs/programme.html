Training School Programme

The training school will take place over five days, beginning with structured teaching and allowing time towards the end to discuss how best to apply these practices to the applicants own circumstances.

This page provides information as to the schedule of the training school, the topics covered and, in due course, links to the training materials.

üíª Topics

Below is a preliminary list of subjects covered for each section of the course. Further details will be provided closer to the time of the training school.

Day 1 - Introduction

  • Welcome
  • Introduction to the ADS
  • Attendee introductions
  • Discussion: Set personal trainee objectives.
  • The FAIR and CARE data principles

Day 2 - Data Management

  • Where to start - Knowing and Organising Your Data
  • ADS Guides to Good Practice and ADS Instructions for depositors
  • An Introduction to Data Management
  • Planning Selection and Retention Strategies
  • The Importance of Metadata - Discovery and Preservation
  • Introduction to Thesauri and Standardised Vocabularies

Day 3 - Digital Preservation and Quality Assurance

  • An Introduction to Digital Preservation
  • Migration and Normalisation of Files
  • Workflows for Archives - Tour of the ADS archiving workflow
  • Discussion - share your digital archiving workflows

Day 4 - Dissemination and Data Reuse

  • Discussion - share your digital archiving workflows
  • Quality Assurance
  • Data Standards
  • Monitoring data and data integrity
  • Where is our Data: Reaching for the clouds
  • Facilitating Data Reuse
  • Looking to the Future at ADS

üßë One one one sessions with ADS Experts

Participants will be matched with an appropriate expert from the ADS to discuss their specific needs and requirements for their organisation or research project. We recommend that each participant come to the training school with a specific requirement in mind, however, we can also help to provide support during the training sessions. These sessions will take place on the last two days of the training school.
